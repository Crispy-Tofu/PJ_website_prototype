Privacy policy

Paper Jam does not collect any data about visitors of our website. We respect your privacy.

If you use our form, the data is sent directly via email to our email address. This data will be stored until we have completed the order and if we empty our mail box. We do not store this info anywhere else. Email is not a secure means of communication. Don't give sensitive information over email and don't implicate yourself or others while using our website. You never know who's watching!

We collect only the supplied email and names for our email list. We don't share your information only with Mail Chimp for our email list. We cannot guarantee the security of your data, once it is collected by Mail Chimp. Read their Privacy Statement for more information.
