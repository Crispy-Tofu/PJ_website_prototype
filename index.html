Print with us

How to print

We are a print shop and you can now order with us online. We print flyers, posters, zines, buttons and books. You can order all of them with us here. We can print all of them in colour and in black and white. Everything we print is on donation.

Learn more about how to print

About us

Paper Jam is an anarchist printing collective that fights against the white supremacist cis-hetero-patriarchical ableist ecocidal capitalist global empire. We print all different kinds of products with different materials from flyers and posters to zines and we even bind books. We work on a donation base so everyone can determine what they want to give according to their abilities.

Along with our efforts to make zines, flyers, posters and books available for activists and anarchists. We are also trying to spread our propaganda at zine stands at festivals and events. If you have an event and want us to be there,

Want to help?

We are looking for volunteers

Paper Jam are on the hunt for new volunteers to help us out. So if you are looking for a chill way to help out the movement, have particular skills you think might be useful for us, or just want to hang out with cool anarchists doing anarchist things, please do get in touch.

We need help with many tasks. From folding and stapling to collecting printers, making books and buttons and much more! Interested?
Send us an email.
