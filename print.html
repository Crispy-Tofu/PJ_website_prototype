How to print

Everything Paper Jam prints is fully on donation.

You can print anything from A6 to A3 sizes and have 80 grams regular printer paper up to 250 grams paper, recycled paper and coloured paper. Most of the options we have available are selectable below. Please fill out all the options, so that we know what to print. If you want something specific, please let us know in the extra comment section. We also ask you to fill out the form for each order you have. (If you want to order flyers and posters for example, please fill out the form twice, as it makes our jobs much easier.) It will also mean we don’t have to contact you and we can just start printing as soon as you have sent us the order.

If you have other requests, questions about printing or want something printed that isn't listed below, send us an email.

Please note that you can only pay in cash at the moment. We also do not deliver the orders unless we specifically agreed on this beforehand.
